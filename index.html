Hello! I am a Master's student in Computer Engineering at Virginia Tech, where I work on reinforcement learning for language models and robot control at TEALab with Simon Stepputtis.

Before Virginia Tech, I worked across three labs during my undergrad. At NCCU Taiwan with Prof. Chih-Yi Chiu on token quantization for late-interaction retrieval. At IISc Bangalore with Prof. Suresh Sundaram on object detection transformers for small objects. At CMU with Prof. Min Xu on 3D segmentation and volumetric scene understanding.
