About

I’m currently MS Student at Virginia Tech.

Previously, I worked as a Computer Vision Intern at IISc (Mar–Jun 2025), where I researched transformer-based object detection and developed a small-object–sensitive detector variant (Hi-DETR) with dense supervision and a hybrid encoder.

I also interned at National Chung Cheng University (Taiwan), where I built a diversity-aware multimodal recommendation system using ViT + BERT embeddings, FAISS retrieval, and fairness/novelty-aware re-ranking.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me reading papers, writing notes, and building research-grade repos that are easy to reproduce.
